VIEWING VIDEO CONTENT

Qualitative Research

Background

Interviews are a type of qualitative research that involves asking a series of questions relating to the research topic to the participant. In the light of qualitative research, it is the taking of data through a series of participant observations, in this case interviews. Furthermore, it is more of a naturalistic approach, as compared to quantitative research which involves the use of measuring things. Altogether, with the use of interviews it can help in understanding human behavior from the participant’s perspective which will give additional insight to reasons and opinions to the research topic.

Challenge

To begin, this assignment will be focusing on two questions based on the research topic of how people watch video content. The first question, “What is your preferred device to watch videos on?”, and followed by the second question “How open are you to new technologies, like virtual reality?” Through these two questions, it will help in determining the current stance of technology today that is capable of viewing video content and the future of it. This type of analysis will be of benefit to a client who is interested in developing, investing, and/or interested in researching into additional forms of this sort of new age tech and see the future that it may hold.

PROCESS

Understanding the Problem

For this assignment, the method that was conducted were one to one face, phone, or video interviews. This assignment did not allow interviews conducted through text form like chat or text messaging. With that being said, everyone in the class was assigned to interview one participant that was under the age of 50 and second participant that was above the age of 50. In the end, there were a total 53 of participants, however for this analysis only 20 participants will be selected randomly from that pool. These participants can be family members, friends, or strangers to the interviewer. This includes 10 interviewees above the age of 50, and an additional 10 that are below the age of 50. With this type of data, it will bring various people of different backgrounds, genders, and ages to this analysis.
