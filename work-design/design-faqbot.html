Our Vision

Step 1: Understanding

Our Users

The chat interface involves the bot (Teaching Assistant) and the user that will interact with it. Furthermore, the product is being designed to be used specifically for students and faculty members of NJIT.

To fully understand how the product will work, we identified the user needs and goals through the creation of personas. In addition, we wrote a chat dialogue based off of their use cases.

Step 2: Research

Our Competitors

Everyone was then given different pages to work on for the project. For my portion, I was in charge of creating a responsive search result page. I decided to conduct a competitive analysis to understand our competitors and how they utilize specific search result features.

I compared features such as filtering, sorting, and the results page. This was to understand strengths and weaknesses for each feature. In addition, to determine how the design will transition to the context of the project we were working on.
